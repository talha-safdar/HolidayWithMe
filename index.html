The most beautiful pictures I have ever taken:

- Altare Della Patria, built between 1885 and 1935 is one of the fantastic monuments based in Rome, Piazza Venezia, its importance is because it symbolises the Roman empire and the Italian Renassiacment. It also resisted many wars from which, world war 1 and world war 2. It was entitled to Vittorio Emanuele II and all this was designed by archaeologists Ferrari, Piacentini and then Sacconi who was the one to complete this work. Nowadays this monument is also seen as a symbol of liberty and secularism, visitors can go in the halls and on the terrace.

- Tower Bridge, built between 1886 and 1894 and opened by the prince Edwards, who used to pull the lever to open up the bridge to allow the steam-boats and other boats of similar dimension to go through. As you can also see the style of the Tower Bridge is Neo-Gothic, this is due to the French influence during the Victorian era in London.
